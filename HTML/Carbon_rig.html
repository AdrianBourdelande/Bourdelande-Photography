In 2018 I began to look for the rigs that existed on the market because I wanted to add that style of photography to my portfolio. After a long search I saw that there were only two options, buy a carbon fiber rig or an aluminum one.
The carbon fiber rig was out of my budget so I went with the aluminum one. The results obtained with the rig were acceptable but it vibrated a lot and worst of all the threads on the aluminum tubes had a lot of play and they damaged very easily, so I decided to create my own rig kit.
I purchased the carbon tubes and added a string tensioning system using 3D printing for some parts of the Kit.
